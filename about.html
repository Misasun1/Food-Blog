How our passion was born


We are couple of friends, a photographer and an artist, who have always love travelling and enjoying culinary experience from around the world. One day, we conclude that the best solution is to share our experience with everyone permitting an exchange and making known cultural recipe, but with a touch of fun and creativity, creating an involvement also for those who are afraid to taste something new, and for those who are bored and would like something different. Because eating is an element that can unite all people.
